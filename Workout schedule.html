Weekly Workout Plan

An advanced calisthenics & cardio schedule for peak performance.

Important Notes

  • Listen to Your Body: Stop if you feel pain and modify exercises as needed.
  • Form Over Reps: Prioritize correct form to prevent injury and maximize engagement.
  • Progression: Aim to increase reps, sets, or difficulty as you get stronger.
  • Warm-up & Cool-down: Never skip the 5-10 minute dynamic warm-up and static cool-down.
  • Hydration & Nutrition: Fuel your body properly to support this intense training.

Monday

Upper Body Strength & Hypertrophy

Focus: Chest, Shoulders, Triceps, Posture

  • Explosive Push-ups: 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps.
    One-arm push-ups, planche lean push-ups.
  • Archer Push-ups: 3-4 sets of 6-10 reps per side.
    Transition to planche progressions.
  • Dips (Parallel Bars): 3-4 sets of 10-15 reps.
    Weighted dips, Korean dips.
  • Pike Push-ups: 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps.
    Wall/freestanding handstand push-ups.
  • Diamond Push-ups: 3 sets of 12-15 reps.
  • Decline Push-ups: 3 sets of 12-15 reps.
Cool-down: 5-10 mins static stretching (chest, shoulders, triceps).

Tuesday

HIIT Cardio

Focus: Explosive Power & Fat Burn

Choose 1-2 options for 20-30 mins total:

  • Sprint Intervals: 1 min max sprint, 2 mins active recovery. Repeat 8-10x.
  • Burpee & Jump Squat: 30s max burpees, 30s rest. 30s max jump squats, 30s rest. Repeat 5-7x.
  • Jump Rope: 1 min fast skipping, 30s rest. Repeat 10-15x.
Cool-down: 5-10 mins static stretching (quads, hamstrings, calves).

Wednesday

Core & Back Strength

Focus: Back, Biceps, Core, Posture

  • Wide Pull-ups: 3-4 sets of 6-10 reps.
    Weighted pull-ups, one-arm negatives.
  • Chin-ups: 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps.
    Weighted chin-ups, L-sit chin-ups.
  • Inverted Rows: 3 sets of 12-15 reps.
    One-arm inverted rows.
  • Front Lever Progressions: 3-4 sets of 5-15s holds.
    Full front lever.
  • Hanging Leg Raises / Toes-to-Bar: 3 sets of 10-15 reps.
  • Dragon Flags: 3 sets of 5-8 reps.
    Alternative: Hollow body holds.
Cool-down: 5-10 mins static stretching (lats, biceps, lower back, abs).

Thursday

HIIT Cardio

Focus: Stamina & Agility

Choose 1-2 options for 20-30 mins total:

  • Hill Sprints: 30-45s sprint up, walk down. Repeat 6-8x.
  • Climber & Jack Intervals: 45s mountain climbers, 15s rest. 45s plank jacks, 15s rest. Repeat 4-6x.
  • Box Jumps & Ropes: 30s box jumps, 30s rest. 30s battle ropes, 30s rest. Repeat 5-7x.
Cool-down: 5-10 mins static stretching.

Friday

Full Upper Body & Arms

Focus: Arm Definition & Posture Integration

  • Muscle-up Progressions: 3-4 sets of 3-5 reps.
    Alt: Explosive pull-ups + dips.
  • Pull-ups (Varying Grips): 3 sets of 8-12 reps.
  • Dips (Rings/Uneven Bars): 3 sets of 8-12 reps.
    Alt: Straight bar dips.
  • Bodyweight Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-15 reps.
  • Bodyweight Tricep Extensions: 3 sets of 10-15 reps.
  • L-sit to Handstand Practice: 3-4 sets of 3-5 reps.
    Strict L-sit holds, crow pose holds.
  • Scapular Pull-ups & Push-ups: 2 sets of 15-20 reps.
Cool-down: 5-10 mins full upper body stretching.

Saturday

Progressive Endurance

Focus: Cardiovascular Endurance & Mental Fortitude

  • Long Distance Run: Start with 5km at a comfortable pace.
  • Progression: Add 0.5-1km each week.
  • Alternative: Trail running for an added challenge.
Cool-down: 10-15 mins deep stretching for legs.

Sunday

Active Recovery / Rest

Focus: Muscle Repair & Flexibility

Complete rest from intense exercise.

Optional Active Recovery:

  • Light walking (30-45 minutes)
  • Gentle cycling or yoga
  • Foam rolling
